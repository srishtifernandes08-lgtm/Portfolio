As my career progressed, the scope of my responsibilities expanded into comprehensive product ownership. At Oye! Rickshaw, the challenge was to look beyond isolated features and take responsibility for complete user journeys.

### Focus Areas
- **Owning complete product experiences:** Guiding features from initial concept through to deployment and iteration.
- **Designing end-to-end journeys:** Ensuring continuity and consistency across complex, multi-step user workflows.
- **Solving real mobility problems:** Translating abstract business goals into tangible, accessible solutions for drivers and riders.
- **Balancing business and user needs:** Finding the equilibrium between revenue targets, operational efficiency, and user satisfaction.
