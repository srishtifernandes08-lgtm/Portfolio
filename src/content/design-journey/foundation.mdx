The beginning of my design journey was rooted in establishing strong product thinking. At MyScoot, I learned the importance of execution within a fast-paced startup environment. 

### Focus Areas
- **Developing product thinking:** Moving beyond visual design to understand how products solve real problems.
- **Startup execution:** Delivering high-quality design work with limited resources and tight timelines.
- **Understanding users:** Conducting foundational research to uncover genuine user needs.
- **Building UX foundations:** Establishing patterns and mental models that would support the product's growth.
