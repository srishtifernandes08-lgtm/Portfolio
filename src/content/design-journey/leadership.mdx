At IDfy, my focus shifted toward enterprise-grade product design within heavily regulated industries. I transitioned from a UX/UI Designer to Lead, and ultimately Principal Product Designer, guiding the overarching product direction.

### Focus Areas
- **Enterprise product design:** Creating scalable, robust solutions capable of handling massive volumes of complex data.
- **Designing for regulated industries:** Navigating the strict compliance, security, and privacy constraints inherent to identity verification.
- **Complex workflows:** Synthesising fragmented, multi-persona processes into coherent, streamlined user interfaces.
- **Large stakeholder ecosystems:** Aligning business leaders, engineering teams, compliance officers, and users around a unified product vision.
- **Cross-functional collaboration:** Bridging the gap between technical constraints and user experience aspirations.
- **Leading product direction:** Setting the long-term design strategy and mentoring other designers.
